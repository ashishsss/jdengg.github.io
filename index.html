About Us

M/s. JD Engineering, an ISO 9001:2008 certified, precision engineering, fabricating & manufacturing company established at Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India. The company is one of the leading manufacturers and global provider of Modern Slaughterhouse Equipments, Slaughter Lines, Slaughter Equipments, Slaughtering Machine, Refrigeration Equipments, Rendering Equipments, Blood Processing Equipments, Solid Separators, Bio-filters and Mechanical Composting Machineries for Solid Wastes. These equipments are manufactured in line with the industry standards and as per the client requirements.
